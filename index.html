Project overview

H2Ology is positioned as a health and sustainability product that combines connected hardware with habit-forming software. The homepage now highlights the core business case behind the product, not just the technology itself.

Business brief

Identified problem

Many people underestimate dehydration during work, study, exercise, and travel. Traditional bottles do not help users measure intake or build consistent hydration habits.

Idea description

H2Ology combines a smart reusable bottle, intake sensors, Bluetooth connectivity, and a mobile app that translates hydration data into reminders, progress tracking, and personalized goals.

Target audience

Health-conscious students, office professionals, gym-goers, and busy adults who want an easier way to stay hydrated and reduce their use of single-use plastic bottles.

Estimated budget

An initial pilot budget of €18,000 to €25,000 would cover prototype development, app design, testing, early branding, and a small first production batch.
